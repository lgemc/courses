# ECON 1640 - Industrial Organization: Theory and Applications

## Overview
This course provides theoretical and empirical analysis of contemporary topics in industrial organization. It examines firm behavior, market structure, competition, and antitrust policy using modern economic tools.

- Oligopoly models
- Monopolistic competition
- Concentration measures (HHI)
